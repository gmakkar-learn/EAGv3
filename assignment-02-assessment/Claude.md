Build a Chrome plugin that helps the user with a self assessment of his/her competency on a specific topic, contents of a book (taken as input), a course that the person may have taken etc. While all options must be presented, for the first version this app should build the capability for assessing competencies of the person based on a PDF document that is taken as input.

The PDF could be a URL link or a local file.

The assessment will be in two parts. Part-1 will be a competency test based on 5 multiple choice questions. These questions must be auto-generated from the context of the material that has been provided. Questions must never be repeated in a session and must test different aspects. We will expand on Part-2 in a future evolution of the product.

At the end of the test, provide a score weighted on the following 3 factors: 70% is based on the accuracy of the responses, 20% is for the quality of articulation and 10% is for the timeliness of the responses.

For every multiple choice question allow a time budget of 1 minute and for every design question allow for a time of 5 mins.

For any area where the score was not perfect, suggest the user to read relevant sections of the book to help improve the score on the next attempt.
